Two projects are ongoing for video integration into the jMax environment :
Video4jmax: a multi-dimensional data processing library for jMax, specialized in pictures and video
VideoDSP: Christian Klippel's MMX-accelerated video stream processing library for jMax. With Quicktime support provided by Etienne Deleflie's vsamp and vfile objects.
